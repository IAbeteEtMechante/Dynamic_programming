Problem Statement

you have N workers and N jobs
You want to assign exactly one job to each worker
Each worker has a cost to do the job.
The cost is given to you as a matrix

what is the min cost you will have to pay?

Constraints
N <= 20


Sample Input
4
9 2 7 8
6 4 3 7
5 8 1 8
7 6 9 4

Sample Output
13
